About Us

Welcome to Kherbouche Designs, where we specialize in providing residential architecture, interior design and CAD/Rendering services. Our mission is to help you create beautiful, functional, and sustainable spaces that reflect your unique personality and style. With years of experience in the industry, we have honed our skills in using CAD software to create accurate and detailed drawings, as well as 3D modeling and rendering to bring your design vision to life. Whether you're looking to update your kitchen, redesign your living room, or build your dream home from scratch, we're here to help. We understand that every project is unique, and we work closely with our clients to understand their specific requirements and budget. Our focus on creativity, practicality, and sustainability ensures that we create spaces that are not only visually stunning but also functional and efficient. As a remote design service provider, we can work with you no matter where you are in the world. We have clients across the United States, Africa, and the Gulf countries, and we're excited to bring our global design expertise to your project. Contact us today to schedule a consultation and let's turn your design vision into a reality.

Services

What We Do?

Residential Architecture Design

Our service focuses on designing and planning custom homes and other residential spaces. We work with our clients to understand their needs and preferences, develop architectural plans, and collaborate with contractors and other professionals to bring the design to life.

Interiors Design

Our service offers customized interior design solutions for your home, including bath and kitchen projects.We also provide assistance in sourcing high-quality products, selecting premium materials, and choosing suitable colors that result in a beautiful and harmonious final product that perfectly reflects your individual needs and preferences.

CAD & 3D Rendering Services

We provide expert CAD drawing and 3D rendering services for homeowners and industry professionals. Our precise 2D planning and elevations, construction plans, and realistic 3D visualizations bring your vision to life. We work collaboratively with our clients to create stunning and functional designs that exceed expectations.

360 renderings

It's an innovative and interactive way to showcase architectural designs. Using a phone or computer, you can explore the space from any angle, giving you a true-to-life feel of the design. Its ability to provide a complete 360-degree view makes it a powerful tool for architects and designers to present their work to clients.

Virtual Home staging

Virtual staging enhances property presentations by adding computer-generated furniture, decor, and other elements to empty spaces, providing a realistic and visually captivating depiction of the furnished potential. This service is favored by real estate professionals, sellers, and interior designers for its cost-effectiveness, time efficiency, and ability to attract interested buyers or renters while boosting the perceived value of the property.

exteriors design

Our exterior and curb appeal design service includes a range of features to ensure that every detail is carefully considered. We offer customized lighting solutions, a selection of color schemes, and the use of high-quality materials to enhance the overall look and feel of your property. We also provide product sourcing to ensure that every element we use is of the highest quality, and in line with your style preferences and budget.

Virtual design consultation

Our virtual design consulting service is designed to solve your interior design problems and offer expert advice on how to enhance your space. We provide tailored recommendations and suggestions to optimize the functionality and aesthetics of your home or office, all from the convenience of your computer or mobile device.

onsite design consultations and supervising

We offer an exceptional onsite design consult service where we travel to your home to assess your space and suggest design solutions that work best for you. With our hands-on approach, we supervise every aspect of your project, ensuring that the end result exceeds your expectations.
